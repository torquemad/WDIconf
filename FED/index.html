SPEAKERS

Emerging technologies for the web. From the inside.

Today's bleeding edge, life changing tool is tomorrow's defunct framework. Development for the web environment has exploded in complexity and opportunity over the last decade. With so much progress occurring every day how do we keep pace?

We gather the brightest minds and immerse them in web development 24 hours a day, 7 days a week for 3 months.
